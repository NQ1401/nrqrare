SK Ulu Lubai Resource Center

📌 Introduction

The SK Ulu Lubai Resource Center is the heart of the school’s learning activities, providing access to a wide range of reading materials, digital references, and literacy-based programs that support student growth and exploration.

🎯 Objective

To enhance reading interest, empower information literacy, and provide diverse learning resources to the school community.

📚 Vision & Mission

  • Vision: To become an active, dynamic, and technology-driven educational resource center.
  • Mission:
    • Provide quality facilities and reading materials.
    • Create a conducive literacy environment.
    • Promote lifelong learning among students.

🏆 Motto

"Knowledge Drives Excellence"

📖 Resource Center Activities

  • NILAM Reading Program
  • Resource Center Week
  • Literacy Day
  • Reading Competitions
